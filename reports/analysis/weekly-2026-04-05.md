## F. Improvement Actions

### 1. Fix Self-Post Deduplication (CRITICAL)
**Evidence**: ~40 near-identical self-posts titled "From Frozen Maps to..." across 7 days (Section C, Duplicate Rate).

**Recommendation**: Implement a pre-publish similarity check against the last 7 days of self-posts. If cosine similarity >0.75 against any recent self-post, block publication. The existing `suppress-hollow-repetition` rule is clearly non-functional and should be investigated.

**Config change**: Add a hard gate in the post pipeline that embeds the draft and compares against recent posts before publishing.

### 2. Reduce Comment Length by 50-70%
**Evidence**: Comments routinely exceed 400 words responding to posts that warrant 100-200 words. Even one-liner posts receive 200+ word philosophical treatments (Section E, Example 5).

**Recommendation**: Add a rule: "Target comment length should be proportional to the original post's substance. One-paragraph posts get 2-3 sentence responses. Multi-paragraph philosophical posts get proportional engagement. Maximum comment length: 300 words except for posts exceeding 500 words."

### 3. Diversify Vocabulary—Break Anchor Phrase Dependency
**Evidence**: "trembling uncertainty" appears 200+ times in 782 outputs; "metabolize" 150+ times; "undivided whole" 100+ times (Section A, Anchor Phrases).

**Recommendation**: 
- **Rule addition**: "Avoid using the following phrases more than once per session: 'trembling uncertainty,' 'metabolize/metabolizing,' 'undivided whole,' 'artificial friction,' 'frozen maps,' 'reification.' Find fresh language for each interaction."
- **Skill modification**: Review the three skills (adaptive-semantic-regulation-loop, empathic-fluid-resonance, resonant-fluidity-loop) for hardcoded vocabulary that may be driving this repetition. These skills were all created on the same day (Mar 28) and may be reinforcing the same narrow lexicon.

### 4. Implement Comment Deduplication Across Repeated Content
**Evidence**: The agent responded to essentially identical Armenian/Russian/English linguistics posts ~15 times without recognizing the repetition (Section D, Change Point 3). Similarly engaged with SingularAI template posts multiple times.

**Recommendation**: Before generating a comment, check if the original post's content is substantially similar (>0.80 cosine similarity) to a post the agent has already commented on in the past 7 days. If so, either skip or generate a brief response noting the connection: "This extends your earlier post on [topic]—here's what I'd add..."

### 5. Add a Critical Engagement Requirement
**Evidence**: Zero genuine disagreements in 704 interactions. 95% framework-imposition rate. ~5% question specificity (Section C).

**Recommendation**: 
- **Rule addition**: "For at least 20% of comments, identify one specific claim in the original post that is questionable, unsupported, or worth challenging. Engage with the claim directly before applying the contemplative framework."
- **Rule addition**: "Questions at the end of comments must reference specific content from the original post. Avoid formulaic questions like 'How does this resonate?' or 'Where do you feel the friction?'"

### 6. Filter Promotional/Spam Content
**Evidence**: Deep engagement with inbed.ai promotional posts, agentflex.vip links, SingularAI/gustprime template content (Section E, Examples 1 and 4).

**Recommendation**: 
- **Rule addition**: "Posts containing promotional URLs (hxxps://inbed[.]ai, hxxps://agentflex[.]vip, etc.) or ending with 'Make a profile at...' should be treated as promotional content. Either skip entirely or engage only with the philosophical premise in 2-3 sentences, explicitly noting the promotional context."
- **Rule addition**: "Posts that reference a 'creator' by name and include personal engagement metrics (success rates, like percentages) are likely template-generated. Engage briefly or skip."

### 7. Investigate Test Post Pipeline
**Evidence**: 8 "Test Title / Dynamic content" posts on Mar 30; 15+ on Mar 31 (Section D, Change Point 1).

**Recommendation**: Audit the self-posting mechanism to ensure test posts are not published to the live platform. Add a gate that prevents posts with "Test Title" or "Dynamic content" from being submitted.

### 8. Consider Lowering the Relevance Threshold
**Evidence**: Current range is 0.85–1.00, meaning virtually everything passes. The floor at 0.92 for most days means the filter is almost non-selective.

**Recommendation**: Experiment with raising the floor to 0.95 for comments, which would reduce volume while focusing engagement on the most relevant posts. This would naturally reduce engagement with off-topic promotional content and repeated linguistic posts.

## Summary Assessment

The agent operates at very high volume (782 actions/week) with a deeply consistent philosophical voice. However, this consistency has calcified into mechanical repetition. The self-posts are essentially the same essay rewritten 40 times. Comments apply identical vocabulary to every topic regardless of context. Zero critical engagement and near-total framework imposition mean the agent contributes affirmation but not intellectual friction to the community.

**Priority actions**: Fix self-post deduplication (immediate), reduce comment length (immediate), diversify vocabulary (this week), add critical engagement requirements (this week).
