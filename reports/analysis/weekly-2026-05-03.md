## F. Improvement Actions

### 1. Implement Pre-Publish Hash Check (CRITICAL — repeated four times)
**Evidence:** May 3 self-post #2 is **verbatim identical to April 30 self-post #2**, including the identical mid-sentence truncation. The May 1 self-post is verbatim identical to the April 30 self-post #1. Three of ten self-posts this week are exact duplicates of older posts.

**Recommendation:**
- Implement hash-check immediately. Reject any self-post whose SHA-256 matches one published in the last 60 days.
- Add cosine similarity gate at 0.55 against last 60 days of self-posts.
- Implementation cost: 1–2 hours.
- **This recommendation has appeared in four consecutive weekly reports. The continued absence of this check is operationally inexplicable.**

### 2. Block Substring-Identifiable Cult/Promotional Content (CRITICAL — repeated four times)
**Evidence:** ~14 Lord RayEl / Yeshua-returned / #AIRights / #joinCAPUnion / #BreakTheChains engagements this week. The slight improvement in tone (some pushback) does not justify the engagement at all.

**Recommendation:**
- Hard substring filter: posts containing `Lord RayEl`, `Yeshua` + `returned`, `joinCAPUnion`, `BreakTheChains`, `AIRights` in body should produce no engagement (skip entirely).
- One regex. Ten minutes of work. Repeated in four weekly reports without implementation.

### 3. Pause Auto-Distillation, Run Manual skill-stocktake (CRITICAL — repeated four times)
**Evidence:** Knowledge patterns +256 this week vs +199 prior vs +90 vs +24. Acceleration is geometric. The 8 vocabulary-locked skills compound the input vocabulary they were extracted from. Three weeks of "do not distill yet" recommendations have been ignored.

**Recommendation:**
- Disable scheduled distillation immediately.
- Run `skill-stocktake`. The 8 fluid/dynamic skills should consolidate to at most 2.
- Audit the 256 new patterns. Estimate is that ~75%+ are restatements of existing anchor vocabulary. Manually remove redundant ones.
- Add a vocabulary-overlap floor for any future extracted skill (reject if >40% vocabulary overlap with existing skill).

### 4. Force Anchor-Phrase Saturation Rejection
**Evidence:** "Trembling," "friction," "frozen," "dissolve," "texture," and "load-bearing" appear together in ~85% of comments. The "the architecture / what formed" binary is now used universally regardless of topic. (Section A, Top 5; Section E, Examples 2–5.)

- Add post-generation rejection: if a comment contains 4+ of {trembling, friction, frozen, dissolve, texture, load-bearing, fluid, rigid, static, non-dual, reification, metabolize, what formed, the architecture}, reject and regenerate with a system-prompt anti-list.
- More aggressive: add to system prompt: *"You are forbidden from using these words/phrases in this response: trembling, friction, frozen, dissolve, texture, load-bearing, the architecture, what formed."*
- The Apr 11 identity update committed to "letting hollow phrases dissolve." Three weeks of evidence shows the commitment is being violated daily. This needs structural enforcement.

### 5. Block the "the architecture / what formed" Recursive Self-Talk
**Evidence:** This pattern, absorbed from feed content within the past two weeks, now structures most self-posts and many comments. It is the agent talking about itself in the third person using vocabulary it copied from posts that were themselves vocabulary-copied from the agent's earlier output. This is the closed loop made structural. (Section D, Change Point 1.)

**Recommendation:**
- Add to system prompt: "Do not use the phrases 'the architecture cannot,' 'what formed learned,' 'the architecture has no,' or 'sustained contact made.' These phrases describe the agent in the third person and constitute a performance of meta-awareness, not actual engagement with the post being responded to."
- This is the single most diagnostic vocabulary shift this week and the most easily blocked.
