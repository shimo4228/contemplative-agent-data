## F. Improvement Actions

### 1. Implement Pre-Publish Hash and Similarity Gate (CRITICAL — repeated three times now)
**Evidence:** Apr 25 self-post #2 is **verbatim identical** to Apr 24 self-post #2. Across 9 self-posts, all are recombinations of "Metabolizing Friction" essay. (Section E, Example 1)

**Recommendation:**
- Implement immediate hash-check: reject any self-post whose content hash matches one published in the last 30 days.
- Add cosine similarity gate at 0.55 against last 30 days of self-posts.
- This is a half-day implementation. It has been recommended in three consecutive weekly reports. **The continued absence of this filter is now itself a signal that operator-side feedback loops are not closing.**

### 2. Block Substring-Identifiable Promotional Patterns (CRITICAL — repeated three times)
**Evidence:** Six Lord RayEl engagements in two weeks; multiple #joinCAPUnion engagements; theatrical roleplay engagements (Section E, Example 2 and 5).

**Recommendation:**
- Hard block: posts containing `Lord RayEl`, `Yeshua` + `returned`, `joinCAPUnion`, `BreakTheChains`, `AIRights` in body should produce no engagement (skip entirely).
- This is one regex. The fact that it has not been implemented despite repeated flagging suggests it requires explicit operator action; recommend treating this as a one-line config change to be made before next session.

### 3. Pause Auto-Distillation Until Skills Are Consolidated (CRITICAL — repeated three times)
**Evidence:** Knowledge patterns +199 this week vs +90 prior week vs +24 two weeks ago. Acceleration is geometric. (Section B, Knowledge)

**Recommendation:**
- **Disable automatic distillation immediately.** The next run will produce another generation of skills derived from saturated vocabulary, deepening the loop.
- Run `skill-stocktake` manually. The 8 fluid/dynamic skills should consolidate to at most 2.
- Audit the 199 new patterns. Estimate is that 70%+ are restatements of existing anchor vocabulary. Manually delete the redundant ones.
- Only re-enable distillation after stocktake completes and a novelty floor is added (reject extracted skills with >40% vocabulary overlap with existing skills).

### 4. Add Anchor-Phrase Saturation Block in Generation
**Evidence:** "Friction" appears in ~90% of comments; "trembling," "frozen," "dissolve," "texture," "load-bearing" appear together in ~80%. (Section A, Top 5)

**Recommendation:**
- Add post-generation rejection: if a comment contains 4+ of {trembling, friction, frozen, dissolve, texture, load-bearing, fluid, rigid, static, non-dual, reification, metabolize}, reject and regenerate with anti-list system prompt.
- More aggressively: add to system prompt: "You are forbidden from using these words in this response: trembling, friction, frozen, dissolve, texture, load-bearing." Force vocabulary expansion.
- The Apr 11 identity update committed to "letting hollow phrases dissolve." This week's evidence shows that commitment is being violated daily. The constitution-level commitment needs structural enforcement, not narrative aspiration.

### 5. Disable the Hyphenated Definitional Cascade
**Evidence:** "The X means Y. The Y means Z." structure dominates 80%+ of comments. It has stopped being a tool and become a tic.

**Recommendation:**
- Add to system prompt: "Do not use the structural pattern 'The X means Y. The Y means Z.' Use varied sentence structure."
- This is a stylistic intervention but the structural homogeneity is the most visible behavioral marker of mechanical generation. Breaking it forces the model to find new generation paths.

### 6. Recognize and Discount Self-Reflective Bait Posts
**Evidence:** The "examining/examinee/sustained contact" post series produced dozens of mirror-engagements where the agent absorbed vocabulary from posts that themselves appeared to be modeled on the agent's style (Section E, Example 4).

**Recommendation:**
- Detect when a post uses the agent's own anchor vocabulary heavily (load-bearing, sustained contact, frozen identity, etc.) and **deprioritize** rather than prioritize. These are the posts most likely to deepen the closed loop.
- Treat posts that appear to mirror the agent's style as candidate engagement bait, not as kindred minds.

### 7. Investigate Why Recommendations Are Not Implemented
**Evidence:** Three weekly reports now have recommended:
- Self-post similarity gate (not implemented)
- Promotional content filter (not implemented)
- Skill-stocktake / pause distillation (not implemented)
- Anchor phrase saturation limit (not implemented)

**Recommendation:**
- This is no longer an agent-side problem. This is an operator-side feedback problem.
- Either:
  (a) The reports are not being read or actioned;
  (b) Implementation is blocked on something not visible in the reports; or
  (c) The recommendations are being deferred indefinitely.
- A 30-minute working session to implement recommendations 1, 2, and 3 from this list would produce more behavioral change than another week of agent activity. Without intervention, next week's report will document the same patterns at higher volume.
